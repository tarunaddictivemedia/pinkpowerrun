Breast Cancer India - Worrying Facts

  • Every 4 minutes – a woman is diagnosed with breast cancer
  • Every 8 minutes – a woman succumbs to breast cancer
  • For every 2 women diagnosed with Breast cancer – one succumbs from it
  • In the global breast cancer incidence and rates in 2022 for women INDIA ranks Third.
  • In the global breast cancer Deaths in 2022 for women INDIA ranks First

The Sudha Reddy Foundation is dedicated to health, child education, and women’s empowerment. Our foundation has a history of collaboration with The Breast Cancer Research Foundation, funding innovative research aimed at preventing and curing breast cancer. Together, we strive to improve diagnosis, treatment, and outcomes globally. In partnership with The Breast Cancer Research Foundation, the Sudha Reddy Foundation is committed to the goal of zero deaths from breast cancer. Through the Pink Power Run marathon, our mission is to raise awareness about breast cancer while fostering a community of strength and solidarity among survivors, their families, and supporters. We aim to educate the public, promote early detection, and encourage a healthier, more informed society.

MEIL proudly partners in national pride with a steadfast commitment to timely project delivery and a strong focus on social and environmental impact. Through the MEIL Foundation, we strive to achieve our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) goals by supporting vital healthcare initiatives. Notable projects include the Special Diet Program for children at MNJ Hospital and the development of the new Oncology Department at NIMS.

UBF With a vision to make breast cancer an openly discussed one, and empower people about the importance of early detection of breast cancer in addition to spreading the message of Hope, Survival and Courage to those who have fought breast cancer, Ushalakshmi Breast Cancer Foundation was founded in Hyderabad as a ‘not for profit’ Charity in 2007. To honour Dr Ushalakshmi's struggle & applaud her strength in the fight against breast cancer, the Foundation bears her name.

Breast Cancer is a formidable adversary, one that affects millions of women worldwide. Yet, it is through collective effort, awareness and support that we can confront this challenge head-on and make a significant difference.

- Sudha Reddy, Founder
